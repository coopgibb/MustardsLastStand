Straight From Chicago to You

Meet the Owner, Dan Polovin

For more than 40 years, diners in Denver and Boulder have come to Mustard’s Last Stand when they want fast food’s ultimate creation -- the Chicago-style hot dog. I opened Mustard’s to bring the Chicago hot dog experience home to Colorado.

We look forward to the chance to serve you some Chicago classics -- and some local favorites you won’t find anywhere else!


Looking for vegetarian or gluten-free options? We’ve got plenty of those too. Come by, have a bite and say hello!
