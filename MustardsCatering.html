Catering

Family reunion? Kids birthday party? Office picnic?

Any gathering is perfect for thrilling your friends and guests with the genuine taste of Chicago. We cater events in the Denver and Boulder metro areas, offering you two options for serving up an unforgettable feast for your corporate event or personal celebration.

Delivered Catering

We prepare the food in our store, show up with hot containers and set up your condiment bar and steam tables. ($350 minimum purchase + $50 minimum setup fees + tax)

Prepared On Site

We’ll set up a working Chicago hot dog stand at your event, cooking your choice of menu items for your guests. ($1,500 minimum food purchase + $50 minimum setup fees + tax)

Ready to Order?

Plan your party using our downloadable custom catering planner, then call us at 303-444-5841 or email us.

NOTE: Your order isn’t placed until we have confirmed it with you. Place your order at least 2 weeks before your event.

The Mini Dog Cart

We’ll bring the hot dog stand to you! This package includes (per person): one Chicago-style hot dog served on a steamed poppy seed bun, potato chips, condiments, choice of salad and a cookie.

The Picnic Gourmet *

Charbroiled burgers the easy way, perfect for a picnic or quick meal at home. This package includes (per person): one 1/4 lb. charbroiled hamburger served on a fresh sesame seed bun, potato chips, condiments, choice of salad and a cookie.

Italian Special *

So authentic you’ll think you’re in Chitown! This package includes (per person): one Italian Beef (with gravy, cooked green peppers and hot peppers on fresh French roll), potato chips, choice of salad and a cookie.

Cicero Combo

Want more meat with your meat? This is a meat lover’s delight! This package includes (per person): one Italian beef, one Italian sausage (served with hot beef gravy, cooked green peppers and hot peppers piled on a French roll), potato chips, condiments, choice of salad and a cookie.

Veggie Party

No meat, but all of the great Mustard’s taste! This package includes (per person): one veggie dog or veggie burger, potato chips, condiments, choice of salad and a cookie. Add a side of our veggie chili for $3.25 per person.

The Kiddie Dog Cart

For the little ones (or the young at heart!). This package includes (per person): one hot dog (with mustard and ketchup), potato chips and the choice of a cookie or applesauce.

The Windy City

We’ll bring that Windy City taste to you! This package includes (per person): one Chicago-style hot dog, one Polish Sausage, condiments, choice of salad and a cookie.

*These items may be served raw or undercooked, or contain raw or undercooked ingredients. Consuming raw or undercooked meats, poultry, seafood, shellfish, or eggs may increase your risk of foodborne illness.
